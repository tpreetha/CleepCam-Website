Facial Recognition

CleepCam uses facial recognition technology to recognize family members and intelligently monitor your home, alerting you immediately if a stranger pops up. Be it your young ones or your pets, you can now secure your house and forget your worries with the smart home camera.

Self-Learning Mechanism

CleepCam is 'Machine Learning complaint' which means that over time, this intelligent video monitoring system will learn more about your home and regular habits on its own This helps in providing you with the best user experience, customized to your preferences.

HomeFit Feature

Use the home fit feature to connect with other smart devices in your home like the lighting or thermostat. Give your home the intuitive power to switch these devices to personalized modes depending on who is at home.

Image

Personalized Alerts

A smart home monitoring sysyem also means generating loads of unwanted messages. But with CleepCam, you can get personalized notifications with images of loved ones without having to scroll through the unwanted messages.
